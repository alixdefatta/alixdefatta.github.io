The Legal Process

Understanding the Legal Process
After a Personal Injury

Going through the legal process after a personal injury can feel overwhelming. At DeFatta Law Firm, we guide you through every step so you always know what to expect.

1

Initial Consultation

Our first step is to meet with you — at no cost — to discuss the details of your accident, evaluate the merits of your case, and outline your legal options. This is your opportunity to ask questions and understand the road ahead. We never pressure you, and there is no obligation to hire us.

2

Investigation

Our attorneys conduct a thorough investigation of the accident — gathering police reports, witness statements, medical records, surveillance footage, and any other available evidence to build the strongest possible case on your behalf. No detail is overlooked.

3

Filing a Claim

Once we have sufficient evidence, we file a personal injury claim against the at-fault party's insurance company. This involves preparing detailed legal documentation and compelling arguments to support your claim for full compensation.

4

Negotiation

Our experienced attorneys negotiate fiercely with the insurance company to reach a fair settlement — covering medical expenses, lost wages, pain and suffering, property damage, and any other related costs. We never accept a lowball offer without your full informed consent.

5

Litigation

If a fair settlement cannot be reached, DeFatta Law Firm is fully prepared to take your case to court. Our seasoned litigators will advocate fiercely on your behalf — presenting a compelling case to achieve the best possible outcome for you and your family.

Common Questions

Personal Injury FAQs

Navigating a personal injury claim can be confusing. Here are the questions our clients ask most often — answered with honesty and clarity.

What should I do immediately after a car accident in Huntsville, AL? +
Your first step should always be to ensure your safety and seek medical attention — even if you feel fine, as some injuries may not be immediately apparent. Document the scene with photos, gather witness information, and report the accident to the police. Then consult a car accident lawyer at DeFatta Law Firm as soon as possible to protect your legal rights and avoid costly mistakes.
How do I know if I have a valid personal injury claim in Alabama? +
The validity of your claim depends on several factors, including the presence of negligence, the extent of your injuries, and the impact on your life. A personal injury lawyer at DeFatta Law Firm can assess your case's specifics to determine its strength and potential for compensation in a free, confidential consultation.
What types of compensation can I recover in a personal injury case? +
Personal injury victims can seek compensation for medical expenses, lost wages, pain and suffering, property damage, loss of enjoyment of life, and more. In cases involving gross negligence, punitive damages may also be available. DeFatta Law Firm will evaluate your case and pursue the full scope of compensation available under Alabama law.
How long do I have to file a personal injury lawsuit in Alabama? +
Alabama's statute of limitations typically allows two years from the date of the injury to file a lawsuit. However, exceptions can apply, so it is crucial to consult with a Huntsville personal injury lawyer as soon as possible to ensure your claim is filed within the legal timeframe. Missing this deadline can permanently bar your claim.
Should I accept the insurance company's settlement offer? +
It is advisable to consult with a personal injury attorney before accepting any settlement offer. Insurance companies often aim to minimize payouts, and an experienced lawyer can negotiate to ensure the offer fairly compensates you for all of your losses — including future medical costs and long-term impacts on your quality of life.
What if I am partially at fault for the accident in Alabama? +
Alabama follows a strict contributory negligence rule, meaning if you are found even slightly at fault, you may be barred from recovering compensation. This makes it critical to consult a Huntsville injury lawyer immediately. DeFatta Law Firm will investigate the facts and build the strongest possible case to protect your right to compensation.
Do I really need a lawyer for my personal injury case? +
While not legally required, having a Huntsville personal injury attorney significantly impacts the outcome of your case. A lawyer brings expertise in negotiation, a deep understanding of Alabama's legal system, and the ability to navigate complex legal procedures — all of which contribute to maximizing your compensation and protecting your rights throughout the process.
How much does it cost to hire a personal injury lawyer at DeFatta Law Firm? +
DeFatta Law Firm handles personal injury cases on a contingency fee basis — meaning you pay nothing upfront and owe no attorney fees unless we win your case. Your initial consultation is completely free and confidential. We believe every injured person deserves access to quality legal representation regardless of their financial situation. If we don't win, you owe us nothing.
